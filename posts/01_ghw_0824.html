Global Hack Week 08/24 - AI/ML

August 20, 2024

Over the last week I have had the opportunity to attend Major League Hacking's Global Hack Week! This month's GHW was themed around AI/ML.

Each day of the last week MLH and their partners held workshops to showcase new AI powered applications and learn machine learning topics. Their partners this month were GitHub, Oracle Database, and Pieces for Developers.

My favortite presentation was about using the Pieces Client OS SDK, Pieces Copilot SDK, and Streamlit to create powerful AI-powered applications in Python.

I participated in a number of challenges and even created my own chatbot powered by Pieces, which I then reimplemented using GroqCloud so that I could host it on Streamlit.

Link to Streamlit Groq Chatbot

I had so much fun at my first virtal hackathon, and I am looking forward to the next Global Hack Week!
